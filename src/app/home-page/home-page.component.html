Who are we & What we do?

Way2Money is a digital marketplace serving as a bridge between clients (people looking for loans) and an array of lending institutions including banks, NBFCs, Housing Finance companies, Micro-finance companies etc. We ensure you get unbiased credit financing deal from our lending partners at unmatched prices. Our expertise lies in advising our clients in choosing the best & most suitable tailor-made loan products from the market.

 Do we charge any fee from clients for our services?

No, our services are absolutely free for clients, we do not charge a single penny from our clients. We have financial arrangements only with our lending partners (commission based).

 Why should I need to Way2Money’s advice?

We have a very robust & error-free algorithm designed to provide you the best possible deals from the market based on your loan requirement & other details submitted. Therefore, please be doubly sure before submitting the loan requirement & all other details so that our algorithm gives you a sound advice..

 What are the loan products available on Way2Money marketplace?

Currently, we are offering comparison & aggregator services for Personal loan, Home loan, Education loan & Business loan but there are multiple products in the pipeline like credit cards, mutual funds, insurance etc. which will surface very soon on our website..

 Which loan to choose for what purpose?

Choosing the correct loan product is essential for fulfilling the right purpose. For example personal loans can be used to fund weddings, spend on a vacation, pay huge medical expenses, purchase laptops & other electronic items etc. Similarly, Home loans are to be used to buy a house, improve or extend an existing house, Top-up on existing home loan etc. Education loans are to be used by students for study purposes as the product name itself indicates & Business loans are also to be used for conducting business operations only.

What are the rates of interest?

Rates of interest vary with each loan product & the corresponding lending institutions’ internal credit policy. To check the rates of interest for a particular loan product, please fill-in our very simple & basic online application form (it hardly takes 2 minutes) in order to get quotes from our lending partners instantly.
